Refund Policy

How refunds work for digital resources purchased from Inclusive Materials.

Our resources are delivered digitally. Once a file is made available for download or access, you have received the benefit of the purchase.

2. General policy

Except where applicable law requires otherwise, all sales of digital products are final. We do not offer refunds for change of mind, duplicate purchases made in error, or failure to read the product description or system requirements.

3. Defective or inaccessible files

If you are unable to access or download a purchased file due to a technical fault on our side, or if the delivered file is materially corrupted and unusable, contact us promptly at the email address listed on our Contact page with your order or receipt details. We will work to provide a working copy or a reasonable remedy.

If your purchase is processed by a third party (for example, Teachers Pay Teachers or another marketplace), that platform’s refund policies and dispute process apply in addition to this policy.

Consumers in certain regions may have mandatory rights (including withdrawal or cooling-off rules for digital content). Nothing in this policy limits rights that cannot legally be waived.

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
