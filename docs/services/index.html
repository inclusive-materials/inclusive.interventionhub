Service 01

Intervention Support Programs

Home-based Special Needs Education (SNEd) support for children and families — built on assessment, delivered with heart.

Every program begins with a baseline assessment of your child, included free with enrollment. This means we never guess — every session, plan, and report is built on where your child actually is, and progress is measured against it.

Program

Tutoring Program

One-on-one SNEd tutoring, tailored to your child.

Individualized sessions targeting specific developmental or academic skills such as communication, pre-academics, fine motor, and adaptive skills. Every session follows a consistent structure: warm-up and regulation, targeted skill work, a generalization activity, and a short parent debrief so you always know what we worked on and why.

  • Written progress summary at Session 10, measured against your child's baseline
Schedule

1–3 sessions per week — arranged around your family's needs and budget

Program

Behavior Support Program

A focused 8-week program for one specific behavior.

For families dealing with a particular challenge like difficult transitions, feeding struggles, aggression, or tantrums, this program identifies why the behavior is happening and builds a plan that works.

See how it works, schedule & investment

How It Works

  1. Phase 1: Observation and functional assessment — we establish how often and how intensely the behavior occurs
  2. Phase 2: A written behavior support plan, reviewed together with you
  3. Phase 3: Implementation sessions plus parent coaching on the plan
  4. Phase 4: Outcome review — results measured against your child's baseline

What's Included

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are these services for?

Our services are designed for parents of learners with diverse needs, classroom teachers and Special Education specialists, and other professionals working with children who have learning differences or developmental needs.

Are sessions held online or in person?

Sessions are currently offered online via video call, making it accessible no matter where you are. In-person arrangements may be available depending on location — reach out through our contact page to discuss.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out through our Contact page with a brief description of what you're looking for. We'll get back to you within 1–3 business days to discuss next steps and schedule your first session.

What is the difference between consultation and training?

Consultation is advice-focused and open to a wider audience — schools and school administrators, parents, teachers, or specialists — who come with a specific problem or decision and leave with expert guidance and a clear plan. Training is a more intensive, ongoing partnership specifically for teachers, where we equip you with your own skills and confidence to support your learner directly, day to day.

Can I combine services with digital resources from the shop?

Absolutely — many clients find that combining personalized services with our downloadable resources gives the best results. During consultation or training, we can recommend specific resources from the shop that are the right fit for your learner.

Not sure which service is right for you?

Reach out and tell us about your learner — we'll help you find the best starting point.
